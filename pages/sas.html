SAS Programming Services for Clinical Trials

Our SAS programming services lay the groundwork for high-quality, compliant data analysis and reporting in clinical trials. With extensive expertise, our team processes data efficiently and accurately, adhering to industry standards like CDISC. We offer tailored programming solutions to meet the specific needs of each trial. Our services include detailed CRF annotation and review for accurate data collection, development of submission-ready SDTM and ADaM datasets, automated edit check programming, and custom macro development for enhanced efficiency. We also prepare pharmacokinetic and pharmacodynamic datasets, create integrated data packages for regulatory submission, and develop comprehensive patient profiles and safety reports to support clinical decision-making. Additionally, we provide CDISC-compliant documentation, including Define.xml and other necessary materials.
